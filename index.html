Instructions
Gather 'round the campfire and let's tell a story!
  • Enter each of the 4 players' names below and press Start when ready.

  • A player will be selected at random and have 30 seconds to write a piece of the story.

  • Once the 30 seconds are up, the other players will have a chance to veto the contribution if they think it will take the story down too dark a path (the vote must be unanimous)!

  • After voting, the next player will have a chance to contribute to the story. This continues until time is up!

  • Once the final timer ends, players will be able to see their full story and then play again!

  • The game lasts 3 minutes.
